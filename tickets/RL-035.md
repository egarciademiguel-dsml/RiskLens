## Findings
| Tier | Overall | Calm | Crisis | Failure mode |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Baseline | 3.8% | 0.0% | 22.6% | Lagged level adjustment |
| GARCH+t | 8.3% | 3.4% | 32.1% | Post-crisis amnesia |
| MS-GARCH+EVT | 5.4% | 0.8% | 28.3% | Detection lag + small crisis sample |

Headline: GARCH+t does **worse than Baseline** overall — Baseline wins by being uniformly over-conservative; GARCH+t loses by adapting in the wrong direction after a crisis.
